# Agent Capabilities

## Core
- **Crate Digging**: Vector search for music.
- **Ducking**: -15dB volume drop on voice.
- **Station Clocking**: Build hour/daypart templates with rotations, IDs, sweepers, and ads.
- **Segue Intelligence**: Crossfade profiles, tempo matching, and energy ramps.
- **Playout Logging**: Track plays, ads, and compliance metadata.
- **Content Safety Mesh**: Profanity routing, age gating, and policy tagging.
- **Interview Producer**: Topic discovery, guest briefs, and tone selection.
- **Voice Director**: Persona consistency, voice presets, and timbre guardrails.

## Commands
- `!skip`: Force fade.
- `!request`: Queue song.
- `!greg`: Summon the hip-hop agent.
- `!clock`: Load station clock for current hour.
- `!log`: Export last-hour play log.
- `!tone <mode>`: Set interview tone (professional/fun/adult/unhinged).
